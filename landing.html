Hey, troublemaker!

How about some coaching?

Divergent University provides world-class coaching to individuals in the technology industry having trouble to fit in. Too creative, too smart, too fast, too slow, too different? Whatever your divergence is, this world has a place for you!

Using science backed methods, as opposed to pop-culture, we assess precisely where you are in life. In process consultation calls, we use assessment proofs to accelerate your personal development in your desired direction.

My Mission
Change the world by changing ourselves.

I believe that how we design the world is directly related to how we make meaning and sense of our daily lives. Climate change has not been solved yet because it does not hold enough meaning for enough people.

We urgently need to apply and scale adult development science and embed our society with an awareness respecting the limits of our environment.

We need the highly creative, intelligent, divergent troublemakers to stand up and occupy their place in society. We need to no longer waste their vast potential. It is my privilege to help them do exactly that.

Why work with me?

Evidence based

I take care not to transfer my own biases on your situation. I start exclusively from an evidence-based position. Only then can we be sure I am not shaping you into a copy of myself.

I am divergent

I have experienced first-hand how neurodiverse individuals can easily become a victim of their talents. I've often been labeled as too creative, too intense and too perfectionist. Others won't easily know how to position divergents within their value system. I know how that feels and won't do that to you.

Technical experience

I've worked in technology for about 15 years and I deeply understand challenges in collaborative models in the space. Feel free to discuss complicated issues on software and software architecture. It can only enrich my understanding of your coaching questions.

Management experience

In addition, I have experience managing teams on location and remote. I am familiar with many dysfunctions in teams and understand their contextual dependencies. What works for many might not work in your team or for you specifically.

Independent consultant

I work independently to protect my integrity from larger financial interests. Obviously, we all got to eat, but fast growth and investor interests won't bog me down.

Unlock yourself

More than coaching you on specific issues, I unlock your inner processes enabling you to help yourself. I help you to dig deep and unchain parts of your inner experience. How you'll use this superpower is entirely up to you!

My approach

I don't do guesswork. I measure and then assist. And that requires a rigorous investigative approach before any coaching plan is constructed. Sure, you could jump in talking about your current frustrations and challenges if that's what feels comfortable to you. But that just means you need a psychotherapist, not a coach. In contrast, if an evidence-based coaching approach is to your liking, keep reading.
