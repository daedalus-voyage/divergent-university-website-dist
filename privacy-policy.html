What data do we collect?

"Divergent University" collects the following data:

  • Personal identification information (Name, email address, phone number, etc.)
  • Developmental assessment materials: video, audio, transcripts and scoring

How do we collect your data?

You directly provide "Divergent University" with most of the data we collect. We collect data and process data when you:

  • Register or sign up online for research or coaching plans
  • Schedule appointments with us
  • Communicate with us on zoom, such as when we're doing developmental assessments
  • Voluntarily email or communicate with us

"Divergent University" may also receive your data indirectly from the following sources:

  • When somebody else signs you up for our services, like a friend or colleague.

How will we use your data?

"Divergent University" collects your data so that we can:

  • Provide evidence-based coaching
  • Conduct research related to adult development
  • Develop technology to scale and apply adult development psychology
  • Collaborate with third parties on the topics above provided that data is anonymised

When "Divergent University" processes your order, it may send your data to, and also use the resulting information from, credit reference agencies to prevent fraudulent purchases.

How do we store your data?

"Divergent University" securely stores your anonymised data using Google Drive Cloud storage. A separate database links your identity to our assessment recordings and results. This database is stored at Google Drive Cloud storage as well, but is password protected.

"Divergent University" will keep your anonymised assessment data indefinitely for [enter time period]. We will delete your data upon request and after payment of a regular assessment fee if the assessments were provided free-of-charge.

"Divergent University" will keep your personally identifiable data for the length of our collaboration + 2 years. Once this time period has expired, we will remove any link between your identity and assessment materials by manual removal from our database. We may stay retain a copy to your name and email address in previous communication.

Marketing

At this time, "Divergent University" does not actively collect your data for marketing purposes.

What are your data protection rights?

"Divergent University" would like to make sure you are fully aware of all of your data protection rights. Every user is entitled to the following:

  • The right to access – You have the right to request "Divergent University" for copies of your personal data. We may charge you a small fee for this service.
  • The right to rectification – You have the right to request that "Divergent University" correct any information you believe is inaccurate. You also have the right to request "Divergent University" to complete the information you believe is incomplete.
  • The right to erasure – You have the right to request that "Divergent University" erase your personal data, under certain conditions.
  • The right to restrict processing – You have the right to request that "Divergent University" restrict the processing of your personal data, under certain conditions.
  • The right to object to processing – You have the right to object to "Divergent University"’s processing of your personal data, under certain conditions.
  • The right to data portability – You have the right to request that "Divergent University" transfer the data that we have collected to another organization, or directly to you, under certain conditions.

If you make a request, we have one month to respond to you. If you would like to exercise any of these rights, please contact us at our email:

Write to us: james@daedalus.voyage

Cookies

Cookies are text files placed on your computer to collect standard Internet log information and visitor behavior information. When you visit our websites, we may collect information from you automatically through cookies or similar technology

For further information, visit allaboutcookies.org.

How do we use cookies?

"Daedalus Voyage" uses cookies for:

  • Tracking the efficiency of research subject recruitment campaigns on social media

What types of cookies do we use?

  • Advertising – "Divergent University" uses these cookies to collect information about your visit to our website, the content you viewed, the links you followed and information about your browser, device, and your IP address. "Divergent University" sometimes shares some limited aspects of this data with third parties for advertising purposes. We may also share online data collected through cookies with our advertising partners. This means that when you visit another website, you may be shown advertising based on your browsing patterns on our website.

How to manage cookies

You can set your browser not to accept cookies, and the above website tells you how to remove cookies from your browser. However, in a few cases, some of our website features may not function as a result.

Privacy policies of other websites

The "Divergent University" website contains links to other websites. Our privacy policy applies only to our website, so if you click on a link to another website, you should read their privacy policy.

Changes to our privacy policy

"Divergent University" keeps its privacy policy under regular review and places any updates on this web page. This privacy policy was last updated on March 17th, 2021.

How to contact us
