This study explores the possibilities for scaling constructivist adult development psychology following Laske's work to assess and operationalise adult developmental profiles in coaching practices. After an initial study of the CDF methodology, opportunities will be explored to scale assessments by making use of speech and language technology (AI).

We live in a global world with an ever increasing interconnectedness between individuals. The 2020 pandemic has made perfectly clear that our behavior in such environment has far reaching consequences. Countries were scrambling to close borders and reduce risks of outbreaks. But it proved difficult to separate so many interwoven pieces of society in an emerging, ever-changing situation.

What is true for COVID, is also true for how we make meaning of our lives and how we understand the world around us. We are inextricably connected to others in how we find meaning and understand the world around us. Our perceived performance is obviously impacted by the quality of our connections with others and our own internal mental machinery. An entire coaching industry was created on the basis of tensions in our view on and place in this world.

A constructivist view on coaching

A constructivist psychologist views the world as an internal construct that can never reflect the external real world. The Constructive Developmental Framework (CDF), by Otto Laske, is derived from research in adult development, especially E. Jaques, M. Basseches and R. Kegan. It provides a solid basis for assessing the two most important dimensions for adult development:

  • Social-emotional development (ED)
  • Cognitive development (CD)

To be of any kind of assistance as a coach, a solid understand of where a client finds herself over these two dimensions is indispensable.

“The world is internally constructed by individuals who, ceaselessly, walk into their own projection.”
– Otto Laske

Armed with knowledge on one's developmental profile, a consultant can enter into a developmentally deepened process consultation enabling the client to self-assess important issues and arrive at constructive solutions. The client knows best. After all, the coach most likely has a different developmental profile and her suggestions would likely only be appropriate is she found herself in her client's shoe's.

How to vaccinate a billion people?

In a world with big injustice, both in our comfortable individual lives and world at large, we need a tool to innoculate against social media control, online bullying and forced conformality. A constructivist would argue that all of these behaviors are generated from meaning and sense making processes of you, me and the rest of humanity. To change the world, we need to first change ourselves. Developmental coaching already shows much promise in accelerating development. We should realize though, that to scale such improvement practices to a global population, a sufficiently powerful tool is needed.

I propose a scaling of adult development psychology in order to assess developmental profiles at scale. With such profile in hand social media software could adapt to the score in a way that enriches our experience, as opposed to dumbing it down. It would allow initiation of communication between individuals that speak a sufficiently similar language and allows pairing individuals so that their developmental potential is maximized. Such tools would obviously need to be guarded in its use. Results would need to be made available to individuals for free and to businesses that adhere to standards of use and privacy.

Research questions

  • What parts of interviews are automatically scoreable?
  • What kind of interactivity in semi-structured developmental interviews could be automated?
  • Is it possible to attach an interviewing quality score to interview content?

Participation

Participation is open to all adults age 25 and above. Participants will take part in 3 assessments (social, cognitive and behavioral) free of charge. After these assessments, a debrief follows which is equally free of charge.

Brief Data Policy
Your data will be stored and processed in the context of scientific research. Additionaly, an anonymised database will be constructed for scientific use at Divergent University only. Your data will never be shared with third parties. You are free to request removal from the study and database at any point in time in exchange for paying a regular assessment fee. In other words, the assessment work would then no longer be a free service.
